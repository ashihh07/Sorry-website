My love, I am writing this because words out loud sometimes fail me — but my heart never does when it comes to you.

I don't want to be on a different server than you. I want us to be on the same world, building something warm and beautiful — together. Side by side, always.

💚   You mean everything to me   💚

Forever yours & always sorry when I'm wrong,
